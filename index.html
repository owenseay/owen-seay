The KLR Trip
Bought a motorcycle from a corn farmer and rode it 5,000+ miles across the country. Turned the resulting GPS data into a scroll-driven interactive experience with real-time map animation, elevation profile, media overlays at waypoints, and a fly that buzzes around.

Walls of Mexico
I photographed a bunch of walls in Yucatán, Mexico. Each wall's dominant color was extracted and mapped to a pixel. Generative patterns flow, sort, and scatter the pixels across a canvas. Click a color to see a wall. Click the pin to stand in front of it.

Banana Vibes
A completely pointless experiment involving 247 watercolor bananas seeded with a deterministic pseudorandom number generator that gives each one permanent DNA via 37 unique parameters. Seven distinct animation engines with real-time physics.

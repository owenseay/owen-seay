The KLR Trip
I bought a motorcycle from a corn farmer and rode it 5,000+ miles across the country. Turned the resulting GPS data into a scroll-driven interactive experience with real-time map animation, elevation profile, and media overlays at waypoints.

Walls of Mexico
100 walls photographed in Yucatán, Mexico. Each wall's dominant color was extracted and mapped to a pixel. Generative patterns flow, sort, and scatter the pixels across a canvas. Click a color to see a wall. Click the pin to stand in front of it.
